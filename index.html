Nice to Meet You

I am an artist and storyteller named after the Princess of Neverland, Tigerlily.

I've lived in many places, but recently have made my home in Charlotte, North Carolina's artist-neighborhood: Noda

Right now, I am a student at the Sequential Artist's Workshop, working on a year-long comics certification. I am also an engineering student at Thinkful, creating beautiful websites and working with talented creators.

About Me

Growing up in the midst of the internet, I was always amazed by all the different ways we can share and connect with each another. I love being able to understand and deconstruct the systems that are behind web pages. Really, it makes me feel like a wizard that can enchant my own little corner of the internet, and I'm over the moon to be a part of the ever-expanding and evolving medium of web development.

Collaborating with others is my jam! I love the creation process between a team, where we can lift each other up and encourage one another to keep growing. My favorite part is being able to trouble-shoot and learn from my mistakes, it makes me a better coder and builds mastery.

Outside of coding I create watercolor illustrations. I'm a cat-parent of two furry-babies: Haley and Luna. I love spending all day drawing or curled up with a cup of tea.
